# Sprint Retrospective: Sprint 10

## Summary

Sprint 10 retrospective covers the delivered slice `Item 28`, `Item 31`, `Item 34`, and `Item 32` (12 story points out of 15 capacity).

- The sprint delivered all four committed items in the planned execution order after the approved resequencing: workflow-level defaults and permissions shorthand, identifier validation hardening, coverage measurement and CI enforcement, and reusable workflow support through `workflow_call`.
- The sprint completed the largest remaining SDK surface expansion so far while also activating a hard verification gate (`bun run coverage`) before that expansion landed.
- Cross-runtime conformance fixtures were updated for every SDK-surface item, consistent with the Sprint 7 rule.
- Sprint closeout completed end-to-end: the sprint branch was merged into `main` through sprint PR [#31](https://github.com/moriturus/ghawb.ts/pull/31).
